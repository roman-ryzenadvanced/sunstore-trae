Why this trains your brain

Inhibitory Control Cognitive Flexibility Task Switching Selective Attention

The Stroop effect is the gold standard measure of inhibitory control. In Chain mode, the rule switches unpredictably — forcing your prefrontal cortex to rapidly reconfigure the task set. This "mixing cost" and "switch cost" directly train cognitive flexibility, the ability to adapt mental strategies on the fly.
