How to Play

  • 1. Choose a difficulty level: Easy (4×3, 6 pairs), Medium (4×4, 8 pairs), or Hard (6×4, 12 pairs).
  • 2. Click or tap a card to flip it face-up, revealing its emoji icon.
  • 3. Flip a second card. If the two emojis match, they stay revealed with a green glow.
  • 4. If they don't match, both cards flip back face-down after a short delay.
  • 5. Clear all pairs to win. Aim for the fewest moves and fastest time for a 3-star rating!
