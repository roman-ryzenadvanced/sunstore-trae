Why this trains your brain

Working Memory Mental Manipulation Attention Processing Speed

Backwards digit span is harder than forward span because it requires you to not only store digits, but actively reverse them in your mind. This dual process (store + manipulate) is the hallmark of working memory — the cognitive workspace that supports reasoning, comprehension, and learning.
