Why this trains your brain

Dual N-Back is the most-studied cognitive training task in neuroscience. You simultaneously track a visual position and an auditory letter, checking each against what appeared N steps back. Research suggests consistent practice can improve working memory capacity and fluid reasoning — the core of problem-solving ability.
