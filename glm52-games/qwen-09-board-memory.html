Why this trains your brain

Spatial Memory Board Visualization Chess Training Pattern Encoding

Board Memory mirrors the "position recall" drills used by chess masters. You must encode piece locations as spatial chunks — grouping nearby pieces into constellations. This is exactly how grandmasters memorize board positions: not cell-by-cell, but as meaningful patterns. As piece counts grow, chunking strategy becomes essential.
