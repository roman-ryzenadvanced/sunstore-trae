How to tend your garden

  • 1. Pick a chime from the palette — each is a note from a calm pentatonic scale (C · D · E · G · A).
  • 2. Tap anywhere on the canvas to plant a glowing chime in your garden.
  • 3. Tap an existing chime any time to ring it by hand and hear its tone.
  • 4. Leave Auto Breeze on and a gentle wind will drift through, ringing chimes in a soothing melody.
  • 5. No score, no fail — just a peaceful sound garden. Breathe, and listen.
