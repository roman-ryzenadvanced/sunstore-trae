Why this trains your brain

Verbal Memory Short-Term Storage Phonological Loop Free Recall

Brief word presentation taps your phonological loop — the verbal component of working memory. You must sub-vocalize each word to encode it, then use semantic associations during recall. Increasing word count and decreasing flash speed pushes the boundaries of verbal short-term capacity.
